BOSSBase real-image evaluation
==============================
Corpus : 1000 grayscale images, random subset of BOSSBase 1.01, seed 0
Size   : 256x256 centre-crop
Payload: 40 bytes (framed, CRC32)
Command:
  PYTHONPATH=. python experiments/bossbase_eval.py --data <dir> -n 1000 --size 256
  PYTHONPATH=. python experiments/bossbase_eval.py --data <dir> -n  600 --size 256 --steganalysis

Notes:
- Spatial carriers (lsb/lsbm/adaptive/amx) are near-undetectable at this payload
  because 40 B is a small fraction of their capacity (low relative embedding rate).
- DCT/QIM carriers (dct/rdct) are detectable here: 40 B is ~30% of their capacity,
  a high relative embedding rate; they also do not recover exactly on every real
  textured image (97.6% / 99.0%), unlike the exact spatial carriers (100%).
